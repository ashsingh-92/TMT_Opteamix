Overview

This tool helps managers map team members on a simple 2×2 grid of Talent and Effort, then plan quarterly development actions and review progress.

  • Talent ≈ knowledge & skills demonstrated in role.
  • Effort ≈ attitude, initiative, and willingness to stretch.

Add people, rate them, and drag their chips between quadrants. Open a profile to capture 1–3 action points (e.g., coaching, mentoring, or training) and a follow‑up date.

Tip: Press N to add a new team member from anywhere.
